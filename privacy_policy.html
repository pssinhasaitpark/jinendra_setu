Introduction

Welcome to Jinendra Setu! This privacy policy outlines how we handle and protect the data displayed in our mobile application. By using our app, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy.

1. Data Collection and Usage

Jinendra Setu does not collect, store, or process any personal data from users. The app solely displays voluntarily provided information of members of the local Jain community in Nagda (District Ujjain, Madhya Pradesh, India). This information includes:

  • Name
  • Mobile Number
  • Address
  • Email
  • Total Members in Household

All data displayed in the app has been voluntarily submitted by community members. The app does not track, modify, or share this data with third parties.

2. Click-to-Call Feature

Jinendra Setu provides a click-to-call feature, allowing users to directly call listed contacts. This functionality operates solely through the user's device and does not transmit or store any call-related data.

3. Data Security

Since Jinendra Setu does not collect or store user data, there is no risk of unauthorized access or data breaches from our end. The information displayed in the app is publicly available as voluntarily provided by members.

4. Third-Party Services

The app does not integrate or use third-party services such as analytics tools, advertisements, or tracking mechanisms. Any external links provided within the app are for informational purposes only, and we are not responsible for their privacy practices.

5. User Rights

If a community member wishes to update, modify, or remove their voluntarily provided data from the app, they may contact us at sorabhnagda@gmail.com. We will take necessary actions to update or remove the requested information immediately.

6. Changes to Privacy Policy
